FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are credits?
Credits are your method of payment to purchase the use hireGEMS™ Assessments. Some assessments use one credit and some combinations of assessments use more than one credit.
How are credits purchased?
First create an account. Then from the "My Account" page, click the Credits tab to buy the number of credits that you want. All major credit cards are accepted.
How can I see my available credits?
The total credits that are available in your account are shown when you're logged into your account at the top of every page. Each a time hireGEMS™ assessment is used, the appropriate number of credits is deducted from the available credits shown.
How do I receive the results?
Within 15 seconds of the applicant completing their Applic™ or Assessment an email is sent to the email address that you entered when creating your hireGEMS™ account. This email contains a link, which you click to view the report.
How do I view my hireGEMS™ Launch History?
Once you are logged in, navigate to "My Account" and click the Launch History tab.
What is "Launch History"?
After the Applicant completes their Applic™ or Assessment the history is updated. Your Launch History includes: Applicant's name, date completed, a link the report and If the assessment was sent by email.
What is an Applic™?
The Applic™ is designed for fast completion by the applicant on an iPad or tablet. They easily complete it from memory while in your place of business. You can choose to have the applicant complete the Applic™ alone at no charge.
