What We Do

hireGEMS™ What We Do For You!

How It Works

hireGEMS™ gives smaller businesses the power of online applications and pre-screening TraitSet® assessments. On your iPad or Droid Tablet or Laptop or PC applicants apply online in your business or anywhere you are! You can even email a link to a candidate you want to apply and assess.

Applicants complete a short employment application which is called an "Applic™" plus you may have them complete a TraitSet® behavioral assessment that you select from the hireGEMS™ library. Choose the assessment that best fits the position: Work-Ethic-Integrity, Customer Service, Leadership-Management, Sales Potential, Antagonistic-Behavior or Money-Math.

You can choose to have the applicant complete the Applic™ alone at no charge. Or include an assessment from the library for the cost of one credit.

hireGEMS™ Benefits

  • Save your hiring manager's valuable time
  • Identify applicants with higher work ethic and integrity
  • Reduce the number of unwanted interviews
  • Automate the application process making it paperless
  • Reduce time spent with walk-in applicants
  • Improve manager's interviewing technique
  • Reduce turnover
  • Utilize valid and reliable TraitSet® behavior assessments

hireGEMS™ Delivers TraitSet® Assessments

hireGEMSTM TraitSet® assessments measure and predict more specifically than personality tests.

TraitSet® focuses on the worker's behavior.

TraitSet® assessments were designed to use the speed of internet delivery and the power of computerized scoring for immediate and accurate knowledge about how a person will behave on the job. This is achieved using over a dozen "traits" that align with specific behaviors:Work Ethic, Integrity, Customer Service, Leadership, Antagonistic and Sales Potential.

TraitSet® is delivered online and the results are delivered in 15 seconds. TraitSet® reports are valuable for hiring managers, easy to read, understand and use. No expensive consultants are ever required.
