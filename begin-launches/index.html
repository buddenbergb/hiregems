Questionnaire Selections

You can choose to have the applicant complete the Applic™ without any assessments at no charge. Or, you select from one of Applic™ and assessment bundles for the cost of one credit. For management and other positions it is simple to bundle the Applic™ with two pre-selected assessments for the cost of two credits. After making your selections click the launch button that corresponds to your delivery.

Results are delivered in 15 seconds after completion. TraitSet® reports are valuable for hiring managers, easy to read, understand and use. No expensive consultants are ever required.
