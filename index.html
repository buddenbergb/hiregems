Behavior Assessments

hireGEMS™ TraitSet® assessments were designed to use the speed of internet delivery, and the power of computerized scoring, for immediate and accurate knowledge about how a person will behave on the job. This is achieved using over a dozen "traits" that align with specific behaviors, such as: work ethic, integrity, customer service, leadership, antagonistic and sales potential.
